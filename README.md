## Website description
When doing research, I noticed that there’s a problem in my local community, the residence has been struggling about the quality of bread they have getting from other bakeries, this led me to start Theriey’s Kitchen this business was created to meet the needs of the people of my community.

## Website goals and objectives 
To bring awareness and increase sales and the profit of the business:
To measure success of our business and monitor engagement metrics like time in the business and product page
views. These metrics will help evaluate the effectiveness of strategies to boost sales. 

## Features
in my navigation bar there’s home, about us, products, enquiries and contact.
Homepage:
I’ve decided to use an image of some of the products we offer so it can easily catch the clients attention. 
About us
This is my storytelling page (background story) showcasing brand value, quality and good flavour of our products and customer trust.
Contact page:
This is the place where customers find ways to get in touch, using phone numbers, email.
Products/services page:
This where I explain my products in full(cookies) listing with high quality images, pricing and
call to action (“add to chart”, “learn more”).
